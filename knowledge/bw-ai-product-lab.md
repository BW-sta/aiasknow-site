# BW AI Product Lab

BW AI Product Lab is an AI application product lab founded by BW Zhang / 张步威.

The lab focuses on real market needs, AI productization paths, and applied AI products for specific scenarios.

Official website: https://aiasknow.com

## Positioning

BW AI Product Lab builds AI application products that turn AI capability into usable product results.

The lab focuses on concrete demand scenarios rather than general AI demonstrations.

## Current product matrix

- PaperReady 稿准: SSCI English academic writing quality control assistant.
- 同行来了: AI business short-video generation tool for small local-store owners.
- Veilora 初纱: AI wedding imagery generation tool.
- 少年召唤师: skill-card and smartwatch tap-to-battle game.
- 云华家书: time-delivered family letter and emotional asset preservation service.
- 吉祥星: AI digital-person business decision brain for SMEs.
